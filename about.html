Our Story

Baking memories since 2018

A Journey of Flavors

How we grew from a home kitchen to your favorite bakery.

'18

A Sweet Beginning

Founded in a home kitchen in Ranchi with just 3 family cake recipes and a dream to spread joy through baking.

'19

Our First Store

Opened our very first artisan bakery in Bariatu Housing Colony, opposite DAV Public School.

'21

Growing Family

Launched the Lalpur outlet to bring our signature cakes and fresh pastries closer to the heart of the city.

'23

Going Digital

Introduced our online ordering platform, making custom cake requests easier than ever for our customers.

'26

The Future

Expanding to 5 outlets across Jharkhand while keeping our artisanal roots and commitment to quality intact.
